Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you implement a solution?

Implementation timelines vary based on the complexity and scale of the solution. Small residential systems can typically be installed within 2-4 weeks, while larger commercial or industrial solutions may take 2-3 months. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer various financing options including leases, power purchase agreements (PPAs), and traditional financing. Our financial team can help you determine the best option based on your specific situation and requirements.

What maintenance is required for your systems?

Our systems are designed for minimal maintenance. Most residential systems require an annual inspection, while commercial and industrial systems may need bi-annual check-ups. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages to ensure optimal performance throughout the system's lifetime.

Are your solutions available internationally?

Yes, POWWRR operates globally with regional offices in North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, and Africa. We have experience implementing our solutions in diverse environments and regulatory frameworks across the world.
